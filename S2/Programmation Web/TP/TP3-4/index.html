Pinocchio

Geppetto, a poor Tuscan carpenter, accidentally makes a puppet out of a log of firewood that cries, laughs and talks like a child, a puppet he names Pinocchio.

Top Gun

After more than three decades as one of the U.S. Navy's top fighter pilots, Pete "Maverick" Mitchell continues to push his limits as a test pilot. He is tasked with training a detachment of young Top Gun graduates for a special mission no pilot would ever imagine.

Captain Marvel

Captain Marvel fights on the side of the Kree alongside her master, Yon-Rogg. Images of a past she has forgotten resurface when she is abducted by the Skrulls, who try to extract certain information from her brain. After a fight, she lands on Earth.
